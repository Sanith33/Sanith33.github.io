What We Provide

Light Fox Studio specializes in creating high-quality 3D assets and CGI product commercials tailored for eCommerce and marketing. We convert entire product catalogs into 3D Assets or visuals, helping brands showcase their products in a more immersive way. Additionally, we collaborate with 3D asset outsourcing companies, offering flexible and scalable production support. We deliver photorealistic 3D modeling, animations, and product renders to clients worldwide.
